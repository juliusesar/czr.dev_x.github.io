Hello! I’m John Ceasar, a first year student from the Department of Computer Studies, College of Engineering and Technology at Tarlac Agricultural University (TAU). I like exploring programming, web development, and anything that helps me understand the digital world better. I want to build strong skills so I can solve problems, help people with their tech needs, and work on projects that make life easier and smarter. To reach this goal, I continue studying hard, practicing coding, and improving step by step. I’m also a positive thinker and an active learner. Even though I’m not sporty, I always do my best in school activities, group tasks, and personal projects. I enjoy working with classmates, sharing ideas, and discovering new things because teamwork helps me learn faster. Right now, I’m starting my journey in the IT field. This is my first e-portfolio, and I’m excited to share my progress, skills, and experiences. I hope you’ll see my dedication, my passion for technology, and my determination to reach my goals.
